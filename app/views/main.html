The Massachusetts Energy Savings Assistant (MESA) provides a single entry point for Massachusetts residents and businesses looking for the energy rebates, incentives, loans, and tax credits available from MassSave®, the Commonwealth of Massachusetts, and other organizations, like the federal government. If you’re looking for help to save energy and manage costs, searching for energy efficiency or electric vehicle rebates, installing solar electricity or investigating biomass to heat your building, this is the place to start.
