ABOUT THE MASSACHUSETTS ENERGY INCENTIVE AND REBATE PORTAL


Want to be smart about how much energy you use and money you spend on electricity, heating and cooling your home and/or workplace, and operating your vehicle? Want to be more comfortable? Want to help reduce air pollution?


This new Energy Incentive and Rebate Portal is designed to provide easy access to all of the energy saving opportunities available to Massachusetts residents, businesses and institutions. This one-stop-shop will provide access to rebates, loans and solar renewable energy certificates at your fingertips for electric vehicles, appliances, home energy assessments, solar electricity and far more.


The Massachusetts Department of Energy Resources (DOER) has developed this single portal so you can find what is available to you, whether it’s from a state agency, Mass Save®, or the federal government. DOER is responsible for developing policy, regulations and programs to advance a clean, affordable and resilient energy future for Massachusetts.


We often hear that it takes a lot of time to look at different websites and find what you’re looking for. Here you will find a comprehensive list of energy incentives and rebates available to the public. With this portal, you can search, learn, and apply for these cost-saving incentives and rebates.


Search using your zip code and you will be guided to energy incentives and rebates in your area. You can click on a link and go directly to the web page for that program.
